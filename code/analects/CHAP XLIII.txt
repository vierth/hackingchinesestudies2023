1. Tsze-chang said, 'What is meant when the Shusays that Kao-tsung, while observing the usual imperial mourning,was for three years without speaking?' 2. The Master said, 'Why must Kao-tsung be referred to as anexample of this? The ancients all did so. When the sovereign died,the officers all attended to their several duties, taking instructionsfrom the prime minister for three years.'
