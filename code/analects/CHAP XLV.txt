Tsze-lu asked what constituted the superior man.The Master said, 'The cultivation of himself in reverentialcarefulness.' 'And is this all?' said Tsze-lu. 'He cultivates himself soas to give rest to others,' was the reply. 'And is this all?' againasked Tsze-lu. The Master said, 'He cultivates himself so as to giverest to all the people. He cultivates himself so as to give rest to allthe people:-- even Yao and Shun were still solicitous about this.'
